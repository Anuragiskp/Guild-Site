🌟Welcome🌟

We are small but tight-knit casual toram guild with peeps who like to laze around and have lots of fun. We spend time helping each other out with quests, events, farming, guild raids, stoodie, and much more!!. We also offer plenty of services at highly discounted rates or somtimes even free!. Our present guildies have decent food buffs and cooks regularly.

- About Us

Our aim is to create a community of Toramers where we treat each other like Family.

🌟Our Services🌟

Gemruns

Gemruns are done for every good event in guild.

Crafting

Any items can be crafted on demand,when materials are provided, free of charge.

Statting

Members can have their items stated by guild staters for free if they provide all mats.

Refining

Members can have their items refined by guild refiners at discounted price.

Builds

Custom/best builds are provided to members for every weapon if they want it.

information
